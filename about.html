About Me

 I grew up on a farm, where my days were filled with shooting, hunting, fishing, and exploring the outdoors. When the sun went down, my love for exploration shifted to the gaming world, where I developed a deep fondness for shooters and survival games. The pure joy and excitement I experienced in these virtual worlds inspired a passion to recreate that happiness for others.

 My fascination with how games progressed and scaled—while still retaining meaning—became a driving force in my career. I am particularly interested in building systems that enhance replayability and offer meaningful choices, allowing players to strategize and immerse themselves fully. As a programmer, I strive to craft the systems and mechanics that make games memorable and engaging, bringing the same sense of adventure and excitement I found as a player.
