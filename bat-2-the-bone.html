Bat to the Bone

Overview

Play In Browser

Developed and submitted a micro game as a final assignment for The Tech Academy, meeting both the requirements of a win condition and C# scripting. This was a rapid exercise meant to get a concept implemented as fast as possible. I applied fundamental Unity principles of sprites, 2D collisions, player input, and game management to transform a basic concept into a functional and engaging experience.
